Frequently Asked Questions

How do you structure your fees?

We believe in a transparent pricing model that works in the client's favor.

Residential Projects: We typically charge based on Square Footage.

Large-Scale/Commercial Projects: We charge a percentage of the total construction cost. We find this structure creates the most affordable and fair balance for our clients depending on the scale of the work.

Is the initial consultation free?
We charge a nominal fee for our first meeting. This is because our initial consultation is not just a "meet and greet"—it is a working session. We dedicate office hours to offer professional advice, assess your site or requirements, and provide tangible value right from the start.
Do you have a minimum budget requirement?
No, but the design is heavily dependent on the budget you set. We ask that you take authority on defining your budget early in the process. We then work strictly within those parameters to design a project that is realistic and financially viable for you.
What happens if I don’t like the initial design? Can I make changes?

Absolutely. We believe the design must reflect your vision.

The Checklist: We start every project with a rigorous checklist to understand exactly what you like and how you envision the space.

Revisions: We allow design changes up until construction begins.

Cost: Generally, we do not charge extra for design revisions during this phase. Extra charges only apply if the changes require site visits beyond the standard requirement.

How long does the design process take?
The timeline varies by project size. Larger projects require intricate detailing to envision the design fully. We prioritize a thorough design phase because solving details on paper is much cheaper and easier than fixing mistakes on the construction site.
Do you build the house (Construction)?
No. We are Architects, not Contractors. We create the comprehensive "framework"—the detailed designs, drawings, and specifications—that the contractor follows. This ensures the building is constructed exactly according to the vision we created together.
Do you handle government permits and council approvals?

Low-Risk Categories: Yes, we handle the permitting process entirely.

Other Categories: The client is responsible for submitting the application, but we provide all the necessary architectural drawings and technical support to ensure the process goes smoothly.

What types of projects do you specialize in?
Our portfolio is diverse. We specialize in Residential, Commercial, Heritage, and Hospitality projects. We tailor our style to the specific needs of the client rather than sticking to one rigid aesthetic.
Where are you located, and do you take out-of-station projects?
We are headquartered in Kochi, Kerala, but our footprint is expanding rapidly. We currently handle projects across Kerala, Tamil Nadu, and Karnataka. We also work internationally, with active projects in Dubai and Australia.
